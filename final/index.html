Final Description

Triggers

Hover on, Hover off, Click

Rules

On hover the image fades from black and white to color. The box that is over half of the image describing the image moves down and dissapear. When the user stops hovering the image will stay in color, but the text box will return from the bottom. On a click a modal will apear with a larger version of the image and the text description on the side. A dong that I feel best relates to the art piece will play while the modal is open. when the x button on the moddal is clicked the modal will close and the music will stop.
