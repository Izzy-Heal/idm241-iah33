Triggers

Click on the retweet button

Rules

The reetweet symbol will rotate clockwise. The symbol will also change from empty to filled in blue. The symbol will grow and shrink as it spins, ending with it back to the original size and position. This retweet data is also stored by the website, and the tweet is published to your account.

Feedback

The user will see the arrows spin clockwise. It will also grow slightly as it does this, then shrink down back to the original sidze as it completes 1 rotation.

Loops and Modes

There are no loops or modes to this interaction.
