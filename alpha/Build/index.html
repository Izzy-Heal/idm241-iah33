Rules

The reetweet symbol rotates clockwise 180 degrees. The symbol changes from dark to filled in blue. The symbol grows and shrinks as it spins, it is largest at a 90 degree rotation then returns to the starting size. This retweet data is also stored by the website, and the tweet is published to your account.
